Visit Tokyo

Tokyo Dome

This Stadium holds events such as - Baseball, Professional wrestling, Concerts, Boxing, and Martial arts
events. The Tokyo Dome opened March 17, 1988 and is Located in Bunkyo, Tokyo.

Ginza

This is Tokyo's most famous entertainment district. Theres a whole lot to do such as - shopping,
eating, going to night clubs, and art gallerie's/museum's.

Visit Osaka

The Kobe Port Tower

This is a sight seeing tower located in the port city of Kobe, Japan. theres 5 floors and each floor has
something different u can see such as - Awajishima and osaka bay to Mount Rokkō and Kansai
Inernational Airport and also features a 360 rotate cafe thats takes 20 mins to go around.

Super Nintendo World

Located in Universal Studio Japan and opened on March 18, 2021. This section as plenty to do such as -
Racing like your in mario kart, Restaurants, shops for merch and the whole park has interactive stuff so
u fell like your in a game.

Visit Kyoto

Kiyomizudera Temple

This is a Buddhist temple in eastern Kyoto, Japan and was founded in the early Heian period all the way back
in 778. Its definitely a place you should visit if you want to see Kyoto.
You can see this temple in alot of anime as well.

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Located in Fushimi-ku, Kyoto, Kyoto Prefecture, Japan, the shrine sits at the base of a mounted
that is also named Inari. Two things u can see are - The Senbon Torii, which are around a
1000's rows of gates you can walk through and theres alot of fox statues holding objects in there mouth's.

Visit Hakone

Hakone Shrine

This Shrine also known as Hakone Gongen is a Shinto Shrine located on the shores of Lake Ashi
in the town of Hakone in the Ashigarashimo District of Kanagawa Prefecture. This Shrine was oringinally
was at the summit of the Komagatake peak of Mount Hakone but was relocated to where it is today.
This Shrine was also popular with samurai.

Hakone Ropeway

A nice sight seeing attraction which you can see between Odawara and Lake Ashi. The line
Links between Sōunzan and Tōgendai via Ōwakudani and is all within Hakone, Kanagawa, Japan.
If you are not afraid of heights and you are in the area why not check it out.
